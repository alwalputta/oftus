Why Oftus® was created:
Oftus® is created to simplify accessing bookmarks across devices. We have seen many web sites coming up on the bookmarks management, but none seem to help in making it simple. Most of the applications are heavy with huge work flows.

Founders of Oftus® have used this application for years before releasing it to the benefit of other public outside. We hope Oftus® will help you finding the bookmarks in a simple way and when you needed it.

Enjoy your world within Oftus®!
