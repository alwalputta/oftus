  • Register into Oftus® portal with your favorite email address
  • Oftus® will send you an email with a link to activate your account.
  • Activate your account by clicking the link in your email.
  • Log into your Oftus® account - Click here to Login
  • Download the bookmarks excel template
  • Collect all your bookmarks into the excel template
  • Go to Option -> Import Bookmarks, select excel sheet and click Submit.
  • All your bookmarks are now imported into IMPORTS category.
  • Create new categories as needed, by clicking the + sign.
  • Drag and drop the bookmarks into various categories as needed.
  • Select multiple bookmarks by clicking on the icons
  • Thats all, you have now created your world! Its now time to Enjoy it!
